						Incorporated in 2003, Excel Infoways Ltd is an ISO 27001:2005 certified Company. Excel Infoways Limited is a leading customer contact centre providing Voice Based Services in the areas of Collections, Telemarketing and Customer Care. Excel Infoways offer a range of customer care services including outbound sales and Marketing, voice, email response, real-time chat, knowledge management, eCRM architecture.Excel Infoways Ltd is a BPO (Business Process Outsourcing) & Customer Contact Center based in India and registered member of NASSCOM. Excel provide BPO service mostly to clients in the United States and the United Kingdom. Company presently have facility at Andheri (W) in Mumbai aggregating 150 seats and planning to set up a 300-seat facility in Mumbai at Borivali (W ) and Kandivali- Charkop in Financial Year 2009-2010.
