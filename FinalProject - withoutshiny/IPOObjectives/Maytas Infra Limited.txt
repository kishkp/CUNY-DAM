						Incorporated in 1988, Maytas Infra Limited is in the business of construction and infrastructure development. Maytas business areas involve construction as a contractor on a contract basis and Infrastructure Development which involves identifying, sourcing, developing, and operating projects in infrastructure sectors.Maytas construction business mainly focuses in irrigation, roads and bridges projects. Maytas recently entered in infrastructure business with airports, seaports, rail based systems, oil and gas-pipelines, refineries and petro chemicals, water and water treatment, power generation, transmission and distribution industrial construction projects.
