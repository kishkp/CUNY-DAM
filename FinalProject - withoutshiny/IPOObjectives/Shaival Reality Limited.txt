						Incorporated in 1996, Shaival Reality Limited is Ahmedabad, Gujarat based company primarily engaged in providing logistic services for transportation of bulk LPG & Ammonia.Shaival Reality is also engaged in construction business which caters, precast construction of housing projects awarded to us by the Government and private sector.1. Transportation ServicesCompany operate in bulk LPG Transportation, in which we load the LPG, Ammonia from client location and unload to specified plant or at required places. company own 114 numbers of LPG tankers. Company's clients include Reliance, HPCL, BPCL, IOC, Gujarat State Fertilizer & Chemical Limited (GSFC), Sabero Organics Limited, Jublient Organics Limited, Maharashtra Polybuten Limited and LPG Infrastructure (India) Limited.2. Construction ServicesCompany provide precast construction of housing and buildings for Government and private sector projects. Precast concrete is a construction product produced by casting concrete in a reusable mold which is then cured in a controlled environment, transported to the construction site and lifted into place. Most Indian realty majors are adopting precast technology because of advantages like quality, speed of construction, and a value-for-money product. It also helps to avoid labour shortage and time delays. The use of such technology helps in saving up to 64% of the time taken in traditional construction Company have constructed 320 dwelling units in Anand District and 324 dwelling units in Dahod.
