The yarns produced by the Company are used for made ups in apparels, hosiery, home furnishings & garment industry.The Company manufactures and sells metallic yarns, films and grey fabrics under the trade name ‘Lakhotia PolyestersÂ’. Company currently has installed capacity of 450 spindles for metallic yarns with a manufacturing capacity of 144 TPA.
