						Incorporated in 1982, Nelcast Limited is in the business of Iron Castings. A TS 16949 accredited organization, Nelcast is one of the largest manufacturers of Spheroidal Graphite (SG) Iron Castings in India.Nelcast's factories are located at Gudur, Andhra Pradesh and Ponneri, Tamilnadu. Ponneri factory specifically manufactures Grey Iron and Ductile Iron castings. Nelcast manufactures around 200 different castings in SG and Grey Iron. Cylinder Blocks, Flywheels, Flywheel Housings, Housings, Hubs, Spring Shackle, Brackets, Transmission Case, Axle Housings, Exhaust Manifolds etc. are some of the items that find application in the commercial vehicle and in the Tractor segments. Nelcast is a major supplier of auto components castings to Automobile and Tractor industry majors like TATA Motors, Ashok Leyland, Tafe, Mahindra & Mahindra, TATA Cummins etc.
