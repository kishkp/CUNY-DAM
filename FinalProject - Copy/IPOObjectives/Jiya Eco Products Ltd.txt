						Incorporated in 2011, Jiya Eco Products Ltd is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company engaged in the manufacturing of bio-fuels viz. bio-briquettes and bio-pellets which is an alternative source of energy and has the ability to replace/are fairly good substitutes of traditional fossil fuels like coal, firewood, lignite, etc.At present, the company has authorised collection centres spread over surrounding villages to source agricultural waste which serves as the major raw material in production of bio-fuels. The company received various incentives from Government including tax holiday, interest rate subvention and VAT exemption. Currently JEPL is one of the key players across the state of Gujarat in bio-fuel sector and has plans to expand its presence on PAN India basis.
