						Incorporated in 2010, VMV Holidays Ltd is engaged in the business of tours and travels and provide the entire gamut of activities associated with holidays like ticketing, local travel arrangement, hotel bookings, Adventure tours, etc. They also provide the most suited holidays/trips to their clients, which are customized to meet their requirements. VMVH's services and products include Inbound and Outbound Travel and Tours Services, Domestic tour arrangements, bookings of Air tickets and hotels for domestic/international travels and also Rent a Car facility. VMV provides tour packages to Indian tourists for places such as Rajasthan, Kashmir, Kerala, North East and Goa. They also provide provides tour packages to international tourists for places such as South East Asia, Hongkong and Maccau, China, Dubai, Europe, Mauritius etc. They provide total travel solutions including business trip, leisure trip, honeymoon trip, family holidays or any special occasion and events.
