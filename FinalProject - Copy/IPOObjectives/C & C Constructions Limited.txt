						C&C Constructions Limited is an infrastructure project development company that provides engineering, procurement and construction services for infrastructure projects in India and Afghanistan. C & C expertise is primarily in transportation engineering projects including roads, bridges flyovers and airport runways.C&C has executes project in joint venture with BSCPL Hyderabad, whom they are working for the past five years.Major Clients:1. National Highways Authority of India; 2. Airports Authority of India; 3. Public Works Department of various State Governments; 4. Punjab Infrastructure Development Board; 5. Public Works Department, Afghanistan; 6. The Louis Berger Group, Inc; 7. RITES Limited; and 8. UNOPS.Existing Projects:1. Three projects on the East West Corridor in Bihar involving widening and strengthening of the existing single/intermediate lane into a 4 lane road under NHDP, Phase II 2. Four projects in Afghanistan involving construction, rehabilitation and upgradation of various road stretches including construction of culverts, bridges and drainage works 3. Widening and upgrading the existing carriageways to multilane carriageway for a High Capacity Bus System in Delhi.Financial Facts:1. In the years ended June 30, 2004, 2005 and 2006, C & C revenues were Rs. 1,636.12 million, Rs. 1,719.71 million and Rs. 2,117.32 million, respectively. 2. In the years ended June 30, 2004, 2005 and 2006, C & C earned profit after tax of Rs. 282.40 million, Rs. 288.16 million and Rs. 309.03 million, respectively.
