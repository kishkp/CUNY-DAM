						Incorporated in 1986 by Government of India, Power Finance Corporation ltd is India based Financial Institution (Non Banking Financial Company) providing funding to Power Sector projects. The company has status of Nav-Ratna PSU by Government of India.PFC provides large range of Financial Products and Services including Project Term Loan, Lease Financing, Direct Discounting of Bills, Short Term Loan, Consultancy Services etc. for various Power projects in Generation, Transmission, and Distribution sector as well as for Renovation & Modernization of existing power projects.PFC went public on Friday, February 23, 2007 by selling its 10.22% shares through PFC IPO. Company is currently listed in BSE (Scrip Code: 532810) and NSE (Symbol: PFC).
