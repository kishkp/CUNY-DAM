						Established in year 1998-99, Pochiraju Industries Ltd. is in production and marketing of cut flower roses for wholesalers, importers and retail outlets.The Company is located in 23.5 acres land at Satyamangalam village, Hosur Tq., Krishnagiri District, Tamil Nadu, a noted Agri Export Zone for floriculture. It has 6 hectares of covered green house area under which different varieties of Dutch roses are grown under controlled environment.Pochiraju Industries grows the fifteen varieties of Dutch roses and proposes to expand another 3 hectares with the following varieties of roses and other flowers Roses: Passion, Hollywood, Bianca, Polo, Tropical Amazon, Royalbaakara, etc. Coronations, Chrysanthemums, Tube roses, zeberas, etc.
