						Incoporated in 2007, Veto Switchgears and Cables Ltd is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company, engaged in the manufacture and sale of wires & cables and electrical accessories in India. Veto Switchgears product portfolio ranges from industrial cables, stand cables to telephone & co-axial wires, from general switches to modular switches, from ceiling fans to rechargeable fans, compact fluorescent lamps and other electrical accessories.Veto Switchgears manufacturing unit located in Hardwar, Uttarakhand. Veto Switchgears supply their products under the brands "VETO" and "VIMAL POWER".
