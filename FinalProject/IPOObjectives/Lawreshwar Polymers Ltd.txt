						Incorporated in 1994, Lawreshwar Polymers Ltd (LPL) is leading manufacturer of canvas shoes, lightweight hawai chappals & EVA Injection footwear. The Company has successfully established its brand name "LEHAR" in the footwear market.Lawreshwar has an annual installed capacity of 75 Lac pairs of Hawai Chappals, 12 Lac pairs of Canvas shoes and 9.87 Lac pairs of EVA Injected footwear. Company manufactures a variety of footwear like light weight fancy slippers, PVC and TPR footwear, synthetic leather chappals in addition to Hawai Chappals and canvas shoes.

						Objects of the Issue:The objects of the Issue are to achieve the benefits of listing on the Stock Exchanges and to raise capital for 1. Expansion of existing manufacturing capacity for manufacturing EVA injected Moduled Footwears by installing 4 new imported fully automatic computerized EVA injection Moulding Machines;2. Installation of a PU pouring Machine for manufacturing PU Sole Footwear with annual capacity of 6.00 Lac pairs per annum; 3. To open 5 new showrooms in various locations of the country along with a new corporate office.
