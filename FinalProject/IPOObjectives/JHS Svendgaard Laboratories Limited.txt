						JHS Svendgaard Laboratories Limited (JHS) is an India-based company engaged in the manufacture of dental and oral healthcare products.The Company's products include toothbrushes, toothpaste, whitening gel, whitening mouth rinse, denture cleaning effervescent tablet and tongue cleaners. JHS also operates as a private-label manufacturer for many domestic, as well as global brands. It also manufactures and sells toothpaste under the brand name TAAZGI in India. JHS has its production facilities (Unit I & II) at New Delhi, and a 100% export-oriented unit at Noida Special Economic Zone (Unit III) in the State of Uttar Pradesh.
