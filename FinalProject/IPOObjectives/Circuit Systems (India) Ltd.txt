						Incorporated in 1995, Circuit Systems (India) Limited (CSIL) is in the business of manufacturing Printed Circuit Boards (PCB). CSIL has around 10 years of experience in manufacturing PCB's. CSIL's manufacturing plant is located at Gandhinagar, Gujrat.PCB's are one of the leading components which are used for manufacturing any electronic hardware product. PCB's are commonly use in high-tech mobile phones, computer, automobiles, Radars and submarine controls and electronic toys.Major sectors using PCB's are Consumer electronics, Computer and peripherals, Telecommunication, Automobiles, Process controls, Defense and Industrial Electronics Industries. Company also export PCB's to Belgium, USA, Italy, Newzeland etc. countries. Revenue from the exports in year 2006-2007 was Rs 880.91 lakhs.CSIL focused on high technology boards like PCB’s with 4 mil standards, lead free finishes, MCL with complex designs etc., which gives it edge over competitor.
