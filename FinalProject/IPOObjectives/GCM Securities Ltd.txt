						Incorporated in 1995, GCM Securities Ltd is engaged in the business of stock broking activities which include equity broking, investing and trading activities. They are providing broking services in Equity and Future & Options segment. They have membership in the cash and future and options segments of both BSE and NSE. They are currently serving the corporate clients and mainly the high net worth individuals. GCM Securities is intend to expand and increase their presence in the retail segment by setting up of up additional 25 branches in Kolkata, Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai and Bikaner. They are also making investment in various companies mostly long-term and are based on an analysis of investment fundamentals.
