						Incorporated in 1994, Ganga Pharmaceuticals Ltd (GPL) is engaged in production of quality Bulk Ayurvedic Medicines in Mumbai. Apart from manufacturing, the company is also into marketing, distribution of Bulk Ayurvedic Medicines all over Maharashtra.Company is manufacturing the following proprietary products:1) Ampachan Vati;2) Khadira Vati;3) Nirmalaya churna Company's products are available in liquid dosage form and solid dosage forms. Liquid dosage form includes Oil-based products and solid dosage forms includes Powder-based products, Tablets, and Pills.
