						Incorporated in 2003, Talwalkars Better Value Fitness Limited owns one of the largest fitness chain in India offering a diverse suite of services including gyms, spas, aerobics and health counseling under the brand 'Talwalkars'. Talwalkars first gym was setup in the year 1932.As of today, Company operates 51 health clubs in 24 cities of 11 states across the country serving over 55,000 members. Talwalkars were the Official Fitness Partners for Standard Chartered Mumbai Marathon in 2008 and 2009 and Femina Miss India Contest in 2009.
