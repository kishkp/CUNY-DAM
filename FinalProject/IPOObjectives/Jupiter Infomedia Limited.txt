It provides information on product profiles, supplier’s directory & trade shows in India.2. IndiaNetzone.com - an Encyclopedia on India. Company produces and publishes well researched articles for everything related to India. It has around 30,000 articles.3. JimYellowPages.com - an online yellowpages directory for India, which contains 100+ cities and lacs of business listings.
