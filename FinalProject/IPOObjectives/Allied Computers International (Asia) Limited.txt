						Incorporated in 2002, Allied Computers International (Asia) Ltd is in the business of assembling and marketing of laptop computers. ACi undertakes services and maintenance contracts for all brands of laptop computers.ACi has two divisions: assembling of laptop/notebook computers, and chip level repairs of motherboard, screen, digital versatile disc (DVD) and hardware of all brands of laptops. In addition, ACi has a software division that is developing software related to hardware used to perform reverse engineering and fault diagnosis of populated printed circuit boards (PCBs), together with explorations of security-enhanced applications by use of biometric technology.
