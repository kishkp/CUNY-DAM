						Incorporated in 1994, Stellar Capital Services Ltd is Non deposit taking Non systemically important Non banking finance company (NBFC-ND-NSI) engaged primarily in the business of advancing loans and investing/trading in securities. Company is a NBFC registered with RBI to carry on NBFC Activities under Section 45(IA) of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.Company is raising fund for acquire offices on rent / lease in Kolkata and Jaipur. On 31.3.11 and 31.3.12 it made preferential issue of 379900 and 168800 equity shares respectively at a price of Rs. 500 per share and on 31.3.13 it issued bonus shares in the ratio of 14 shares for every 1 share held. Post issue the equity will rise to Rs. 16.56 crore.
