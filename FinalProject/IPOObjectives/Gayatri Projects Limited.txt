						Gayatri Projects Limited (GPL), formerly Andhra Coastal Construction Private Limited, is engaged in the execution of civil works.The Company provides engineering, procurement, construction and project management services for various infrastructure projects. GPL has executed various projects in different sectors of infrastructure like highway, irrigation projects, mass excavation, ports, airports and industrial civil works. The Company has constructed about 644 kilometers of the highways and 1113 kilometers of irrigation canals. GPL has executed 21 irrigation projects, seven projects for construction of dams and reservoir, nine highway and runway projects, eight site leveling projects and three industrial projects. The Company has completed projects, such as construction of five packages of Narmada Main Canal comprising of the 50.60 kilometers and Upper Krishna Project comprising of 44 kilometers.
