						Vimal Oil and Foods Limited is BSE listed company (BSE Code: 519373), engaged in the production of edible oil. VOFL export the De-oiled Cake (DOC), a by-product under the solvent extraction to various foreign countries. VOFL Product Range includes oils of Cottonseed, Groundnut, Soya, mustard and palm. The Refined Oil is sold in the local market under the 'VIMAL' Brand nameVOFL also has a Wind Mill which is situated at village Kalyanpur, Dist. Jamnagar with a capacity of 0.225 Mg Watt. The electricity generated by the plant is supplied to Gujarat Electricity Board and gets the credit against Electricity purchased from Gujarat Electricity Board for its plants at other locations.VOFL has a Milk Powder plant and planning to diversify into fast growing Snack Food and Food Processing sector.
