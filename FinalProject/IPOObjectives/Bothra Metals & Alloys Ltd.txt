						Incorporated in 2001, Bothra Metals and Alloys Ltd is engaged in the business of manufacturing aluminium extrusions, aluminium profiles and aluminium billets for commercial, industrial and domestic applications.Company offers a wide range of aluminum products including aluminium ingots, aluminium billets, aluminium shots and aluminum extrusion products like aluminium channels, aluminium sections, aluminium flat bars, aluminium tubes, etc. Company is also engaged in trading of aluminium scrap.Company currently operates from three plants; viz. Kala-amb (Himachal Pradesh), Bhavnagar (Gujarat) and Sangli (Maharashtra). The main raw material for the products is aluminium ingots and aluminum scrap, which is sourced from India and abroad.Aluminium extrusions manufactured by the Company are used in several applications, including heat sinks, heat exchangers, aluminium doors and window frames. Products provided by the Company is used in growing sectors of Indian economy, viz. Transport, Building and construction, Electrical & mechanical engineering, Packaging, Automobile, Beverage, and Marine. Poducts are also used in window and door frame systems, pre fabricated building structures, shop fronts, exterior and roofing claddings, curtain walling, etc.
