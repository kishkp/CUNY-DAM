						Incorporated in 1998, Jindal Cotex Limited is in the business of manufacturing of Acrylic, Polyester, and Polyester-Viscose, Polyester Cotton, combed and carded yarns, which are appropriate for apparels, suitings & knitted fabrics.Jindal Cotex has installed capacity of 23,472 spindles for acrylic, cotton blended and polyester yarns. Company manufacture and sell yarns under the trade name 'JINDAL'.Jindal Cotex has also installed and commissioned a Suzlon make Wind Electric Generator (Wind Mill) of 1250 KW capacity at Pithla-Satta-Gorera in Distt. Jaisalmer, Rajasthan. The entire power generated through this wind mill will be sold to Ajmer Vidyut Vitran Nigam Limited.
