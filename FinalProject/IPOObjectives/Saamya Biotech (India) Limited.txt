						Incorporated in 2002, Saamya Biotech India Limited (SBIL) is a Hyderabad based Biotech company. SBIL manufacture and market bio pharmaceutical and recombinant protein products to medical and other industries.SBIL proposes to set up world class facilities for the manufacture of fermentation based high value Biotech Products. SBIL embarks on developing effective strategies for the production and supply of both bulk and formulations with special focus on life saving drug entities at affordable price.
