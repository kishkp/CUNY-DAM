						Nissan Copper Limited is manufacturer of copper ingots, coils, wire bars, strips, section, flats, billet bars, rods and copper pipes. The products manufactured by Nissan Copper are mainly used in switchgears / panels, radiators, refrigerators, electric appliances, connectors, heat exchangers etc.Nissan Copper Limited has two manufacturing units. One located at Silvassa, where they carry out the manufacturing processes and the other located at Umbergaon which is presently involved in futher processing.
