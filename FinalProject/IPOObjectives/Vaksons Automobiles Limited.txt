						Vaksons Automobiles is authorised dealer for Force Motors Ltd. Company sell and service Light Commercial Vehicles (LCV), Multi-Utility Vehicles (MUV), Small Commercial Vehicles (SCV Vehicles). Vaksons Automobiles has 3 showrooms of Force Motors at Sonepat, Panipat and Gohana within the state of Haryana. Company also has in-house service centre at Sonepat for the servicing and other after-sales needs.Apart from the vehicle dealership business, Vaksons Automobiles have also been involved in trading of vehicle spare parts and utilities of branded as well as generic products forming part of the auto value chain. Vaksons Automobiles have recently incorporated a subsidiary company in the name and style of 'Vaksons Metaplast Pvt. Ltd.' in order to undertake trading in auto parts, HDPE and LDPE polymer, aluminium scrap, and other metal and plastic items on a larger scale.

						

						Company Promoters:Mr. Atul Kumar Jain and Mrs. Vandana Jain are the Promoters of the Company.
