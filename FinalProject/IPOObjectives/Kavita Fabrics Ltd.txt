						Incorporated in 2005, Kavita Fabrics Ltd is in the business of manufacturing synthetic fabrics from man-made yarns by using weaving technology. Based in Surat (Gujarat), Company manufactures synthetic fabrics in the form of semi-finished sarees and dress materials. They comes in various designs as per customer requirements and further processed by their customers before selling to the end-users.The main raw material for their product is man-made yarn, which is acquired from several local suppliers. Company owns 36 jacquard power looms to undertake the weaving of the synthetic fabrics from man-made yarns.
