						Everonn Systems India Limited is Knowledge Management, Education and Training Company. Everonn provides its services in Creating Educational and Training Content, Designing and executing large learning initiatives and setting up the needed infrastructure for learning and training.Everonn has Virtual and Interactive Learning classroom networks across India. Everonn does business through 2 strong Business Units, Institutional Education and Infrastructure Services (IE & IS) and Virtual and Technology Enabled Learning Solutions (ViTELS).
